# Amino Acid Explorer

## How to Use
- Open `index.html` to browse all amino acids.
- Use the filter buttons to narrow by category.
- Click `Learn` to study flashcards. Click the card to flip.
- Click `Recall` to quiz yourself. Type an answer and press Enter.
- Click `Draw` to practice drawing the structure.
- Click `Progress` to see your results on this device.

## Notes
- Your progress is saved in your browser on this device.
- Clearing browser storage resets your progress. You can also clear by clicking the button in Progress.
